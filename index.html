AI-Powered Rating

Smart algorithms ensure accurate and fair reviews.

Our platform leverages cutting-edge machine learning to analyze and moderate user feedback. By automatically detecting fraudulent or manipulated ratings, our AI ensures that every handyman or service provider gets the recognition they truly deserve. Whether you’re looking to hire someone or you’re a professional seeking fair representation, our system maintains an unbiased and transparent standard.

  • Automated detection of spam and duplicate reviews.
  • Weighted scoring system to favor quality feedback.
  • Adaptive learning to continuously improve accuracy.

Decentralized & Secure

Built on blockchain for transparency and trust.

Gone are the days of centralized control. By implementing blockchain technology, we ensure that data integrity and transparency are at the core of our platform. Every job order, payment transaction, and review is immutably recorded on the ledger, giving both clients and service providers confidence that all information is accurate, unaltered, and fully verifiable.

  • Immutable records for absolute integrity.
  • Transparent tracking of interactions.
  • Prevent tampering or alterations in reviews and ratings.

Crypto Integration

Seamless payments with popular cryptocurrencies.

Embrace the future of finance by offering clients and handymen the flexibility to use major cryptocurrencies for transactions. Our platform simplifies the payment process, reducing fees and removing cross-border limitations. Whether you prefer Bitcoin, Ethereum, or other popular tokens, our integrated payment gateway is built to make every transaction effortless, secure, and fast.

  • Support for major cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, USDC, etc.).
  • Built-in crypto wallet for easy fund management.
  • Instant confirmations and reduced transaction fees.

Empowering Handymen

A platform designed to benefit both clients and professionals.

Our primary goal is to ensure that handymen thrive while customers receive top-notch service. We've developed an intuitive interface that simplifies every step of the job cycle—from searching for tasks to bidding, scheduling, and payment. This not only creates a smoother process for service providers but also fosters a better customer experience. By bridging the gap between clients and handymen, we empower talented professionals to grow their businesses and showcase their expertise.

  • Fair fees and transparent commissions.
  • Ratings and reviews that spotlight quality work.
  • Tools for showcasing portfolios and skillsets.

Flexible Roles

Everyone can be both a service provider and a client.

One of the most empowering aspects of our platform is that you’re never locked into a single role. Whether you want to offer your skill set as a professional handyman or you need expert help for a project, it’s all available in one place. Our community thrives on this dual nature, allowing people to seamlessly switch between being a provider and being a customer.

  • Switch roles at any time—no separate account needed.
  • Expand your earning potential by offering your expertise.
  • Tap into a diverse pool of services when you need assistance.
